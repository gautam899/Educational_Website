Learn More About Credential of Readiness

The Credential of Readiness (CORe) is a comprehensive online program offered by Harvard Business School (HBS) Online. It's designed to equip you with a deep understanding of essential business concepts and problem-solving skills. This program is a primer on the fundamentals of business thinking, preparing you to participate fully in the business world.

More About the Course Economics for Mangers

The "Economics for Managers" course will enable you to drive critical business decisions. You'll explore how businesses approach pricing strategy, evaluate market demand, and differentiate their offerings. You'll learn how to apply economic principles to real business situations.

Learn More About Financial Accounting

In the Financial Accounting course, you'll discover what's behind the numbers in financial statements. You'll learn how to evaluate the financial health of a business and prepare and evaluate financial forecasts.

Learn More About Leadership Principle

The “Leadership Principles” course by Harvard Business School (HBS) Online is designed to help new and aspiring leaders unleash their potential and cultivate high-performing teams. It offers real-world leadership challenges, self-assessments, and 360-degree feedback, enabling you to become a more versatile leader.
