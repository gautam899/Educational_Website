Course Overview

The Credential of Readiness (CORe) is a comprehensive online program offered by Harvard Business School (HBS) Online. It’s designed to equip you with a deep understanding of essential business concepts and problem-solving skills. This program is a primer on the fundamentals of business thinking, preparing you to participate fully in the business world.

CORe consists of three courses: Business Analytics, Economics for Managers, and Financial Accounting. Each course is designed to instill fluency in the language of business. This is the same program that HBS offers to incoming students to prepare for the MBA classroom. In the Business Analytics course, you’ll start with basic descriptive statistics and progress to regression analysis. You’ll implement analytical techniques in Excel and apply fundamental quantitative methods to real business problems.

This course will enable you to analyze and interpret data to inform business decisions. The Economics for Managers course will enable you to drive critical business decisions. You’ll explore how businesses approach pricing strategy, evaluate market demand, and differentiate their offerings. You’ll learn how to apply economic principles to real business situations. In the Financial Accounting course, you’ll discover what’s behind the numbers in financial statements. You’ll learn how to evaluate the financial health of a business and prepare and evaluate financial forecasts.

What you'll learn

Analyze and interpret data to inform business decisions

Implement analytical techniques in Excel

Develop effective pricing strategies

Identify sources of competitive advantage through differentiation

Evaluate the financial health of a business using financial statements

Prepare and evaluate financial forecasts
